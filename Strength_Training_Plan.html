Week 1 to 4 Plan

Day 1: Deadlift and Overhead Press Focus

Deadlift: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 120 kg (increase by 2.5-5 kg each week)

Overhead Press: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 45 kg (increase by 2.5 kg each week)

Pull-ups: 3 sets x as many reps as possible

Plank: 3 sets x 1 min

Day 2: Squat Focus

Squats: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 100 kg (increase by 2.5-5 kg each week)

Bench Press: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 75 kg (increase by 2.5 kg each week)

Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets x 8 reps (lighter weight, focus on form)

Leg Curls: 3 sets x 10 reps

Day 3: Bench Press and Accessory Work

Bench Press: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 75 kg (increase by 2.5 kg each week)

Overhead Press: 4 sets x 5 reps @ 45 kg (as in Day 1)

Deadlift: 3 sets x 5 reps @ 120 kg (lighter than Day 1)

Ab Wheel Rollouts: 3 sets x 10 reps
